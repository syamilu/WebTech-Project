Security

television (CCTV) systems play a crucial role in maintaining security within student residences.

Strategically placed cameras help monitor common areas, entrances, and exits, providing real-time surveillance and recording any suspicious activities

CCTV systems serve as deterrents and are indispensable in addressing security concerns within the premises.

Internet

Students living in on-campus residences can benefit from a dedicated internet connection specifically designed to meet their academic needs, The internet facilities in students' residences are regularly monitored and maintained by IT professionals.

Connections are often provided by the university or college, ensuring that students have access to uninterrupted internet service throughout their stay.

Students' residences often provide Wi-Fi connectivity, extending the reach of the internet throughout the entire building. This enables students to connect their laptops, smartphones, and other devices to the network without the need for wired connections
